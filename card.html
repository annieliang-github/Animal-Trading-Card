Maine Coon Cat

The Maine Coon was made the official state cat of Maine in 1985. But they're also beloved by cat lovers across America.

  • Weight range: 8-12 lbs
  • Eye color: Copper, Green, Gold
  • Longevity Range: 9-13 yrs
  • Attention Needs: Moderate, High

Maine Coons cats are known as the "gentle giants" and possess above-average intelligence, making them easy to train. They are known for being loyal to their family, but are independent and not clingy.
